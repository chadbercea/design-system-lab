## Camera Movements
**Dolly:** Move closer/farther
**Orbital:** 270Â° rotation around subject
**Tracking:** Follow subject motion
**Top-down:** Descending bird's eye

## Animation Curves
**Ease In-Out:** Smooth accel/decel (default)
**Ease Out:** Quick start, gentle stop (landings)
**Ease In:** Gentle start, quick finish (launches)

## Key Moments
1. **0:02 "The Spark"** - Wireframe first appears
2. **0:10 "The Launch"** - Package takes off
3. **0:18 "The Dock"** - Click into place
4. **0:25 "The Seal"** - Doors close

## Sound Design
Build: Low hum, periodic chunks, rising pitch
Transport: Whoosh launch, wind ambience, deceleration
Seal: Dock clunk, hydraulic hiss, ready tone

## Production Tips
1. Keyframes first, timing second
2. Consistent easing for similar motions
3. Test on target device
4. Add sound early
5. Get feedback often
